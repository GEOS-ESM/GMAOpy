.. _geography:

geography
=========

The geography directive describes the projection required and aggregates different attributes. The keywords in geography match mostly the definition of the method **basemap** in matplotlib. Other attributes match different function of the basemap module: coastlines,  continents,  boundary,  countries,  states,  rivers,  parallels,  meridians.    http://matplotlib.github.com/basemap/api/basemap_api.html?highlight=continents#mpl_toolkits.basemap.Basemap
